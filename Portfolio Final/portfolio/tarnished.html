TARNISHED

Game Description

A VR horror Escape room Experience, that is what Tarnished is.

You're locked in a prison cell in an abandoned part of Russia after the nukes dropped. And have been locked in there for a few months now. But since the bombs dropped and everyone left, you feel watched... Like someone is or something is coming closer each minute.

It is your job to get out of there by solving the puzzles before IT eventually finds you and brings your life to a gruesome end.

Will you be able to survive?

Development

Development on Tarnished started in Febuary.

The idea was to create a horror vr experience in 2 months time.

Process started with analysing existing Horror games like: Amnesia, I expect you to die, Chair in a Room, etc.

I did the Game design. I designed the puzzle, the level and the concept. My friend did the 3D modeling and concept arting

I worked with the SteamVR SDK

The Entire project was made in Unity3D and code editor Visual Studio 2019
